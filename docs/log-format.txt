Log file (CSV) entry formats (Version of 23-Apr-14) -CR

<DATE>,<TIME>,<TYPE>,...

* <DATE> and <TIME> in format automatically recognized by Excel, e.g.,

  4/23/14,09:15:33,...

* <TYPE> = SESSION or ACTIVITY

-------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,SESSION,ATTEMPTED,<STATE>

* <STATE> = ATTENTION or INITIATING
            (state reached before going back to idle)
            (there will be no such entries for login condition)

<DATE>,<TIME>,SESSION,START
<DATE>,<TIME>,SESSION,END,<DURATION>,<REPEATS>,<DISENGAGMENT>

* <DURATION> in minutes
* <REPEATS> number of times user clicked "What did you say?"
* <DISENGAGEMENT> = GOODBYE or TIMEOUT

<DATE>,<TIME>,SESSION,CLOSENESS,<LEVEL>

* <LEVEL> = STRANGER or ACQUAINTANCE or COMPANION

<DATE>,<TIME>,SESSION,REMINDER,<EVENT>

* <EVENT> = "..."

<DATE>,<TIME>,SESSION,AGENT,<UTTERANCE>
<DATE>,<TIME>,SESSION,MENU,<CHOSEN>,<CHOICES>...

* <UTTERANCE> = "..."

* <CHOSEN> = "..."

* <CHOICES> = "...",...

---------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,<PLUGIN>,...

* <PLUGIN> = ABOUT or
             ANECDOTES or
             CALENDAR or 
             CHECKERS or 
             ENROLL or
             EXERCISE or
             EXPLAIN or
             GREETINGS or 
             HEALTH or
             NUTRITION or
             RUMMY or 
             SKYPE or
             STORY or 
             TTT or
             WEATHER

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,<PLUGIN>,PROPOSED,<ACCEPTED>

* <ACCEPTED> = ACCEPTED or NOT-ACCEPTED

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,<PLUGIN>,START
<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,<PLUGIN>,END,<DURATION>,<REPEATS>

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,AGENT,<UTTERANCE>
<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,MENU,<CHOSEN>,<CHOICES>...

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,ABOUT,<POSITIVE>,<NEGATIVE>,<EXIT>

<POSITIVE> = POSITIVE or NOT-POSITIVE
<NEGATIVE> = NEGATIVE or NOT-NEGATIVE
<EXIT> = EARLY or NORMAL

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,ANECDOTES,<TITLE>,<FINISHED>

* <TITLE> = "..."
* <FINISHED> = FINISHED or NOT-FINISHED

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,CALENDAR,EVENT,<TITLE>

* <TITLE> = "..." (see details of event in user model printout)

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,CALENDAR,VIEW,<WEEK>

* <WEEK> = CURRENT or NEXT

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,CHECKERS,<WON>,<JUMPS>,<DOUBLES>

* <WON> = USER or AGENT or NOT (finished)
* <JUMPS> = total number of jumps in game
* <DOUBLES> = total number of double jumps in game

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,ENROLL,PERSON,<NAME>

* <NAME> = "..."  (see details of person in user model printout)

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,EXERCISE,<GOALS>,<ACTIVITIES>,<SERVINGS>

* <GOALS> = GOALS or NOT-GOALS
* <ACTIVITIES> = ACTIVITIES or NOT-ACTIVITIES
* <SERVINGS> = SERVINGS or NOT-SERVINGS

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,EXPLAIN,<PLUGIN>

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,GREETINGS,<TOD>

* <TOD> = MORNING or AFTERNOON or EVENING or NIGHT

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,HEALTH,...

* TBD

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,NUTRITION,...

* TBD

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,RUMMY,<WON>,<MELDS>

* <MELDS> = total number of melds in game

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,SKYPE,CALL,<WHO>,<DURATION>,<APPT>,<DIRECTION>

* <WHO> = Skype name
* <APPT> = APPT or NOT-APPT
* <DIRECTION> = INCOMING or OUTGOING

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,STORY,TOLD,<TITLE>,<DURATION>,<SAVED>,<SENT>

* <TITLE> = "..."
* <SAVED> = SAVED or NOT-SAVED
* <SENT> = SENT or NOT-SENT

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,TTT,<WON>,<FIRST>

* <FIRST> = USER or AGENT

---------------------------------------------------------

<DATE>,<TIME>,ACTIVITY,WEATHER,<CITIES>,<FRIENDS>

* <CITIES> = CITIES or NOT-CITIES
* <FRIENDS> = FRIENDS or NOT-FRIENDS

---------------------------------------------------------
